# Product Image Rules

Use this checklist whenever adding or updating a product listing, PDP, bundle, or Printful-backed SKU.

## Concept And Printful Workflow

1. Brainstorm a concept that fits the selected product category.
2. The storefront logo is optional and should only be included when explicitly requested.
3. Prioritize an original, product-appropriate American 1980s-1990s concept.
4. Generate the concept on a perfectly flat green chroma-key background.
5. Remove the green background and export the concept as a transparent PNG.
6. Validate that the transparent artwork has clean edges and no remaining green field.
7. Apply only the transparent artwork to the selected product in Printful.
8. Download every useful product mockup angle Printful provides.
9. Remove the background surrounding each downloaded Printful mockup.
10. Center the isolated product inside a `1:1` frame without clipping or distortion.
11. Use the background-removed concept PNG as the required primary artwork reference for lifestyle generation.
12. Use the Printful mockup as a secondary reference for the physical product, proportions, material, and print placement.

The green concept background is temporary chroma-key only. Never upload or print the concept while the green background is still present.

## Required Image Set

Each product should have these image groups when possible:

1. Primary product mockup
   - Use a clean product-focused image.
   - Remove raw white background into transparency when the image sits on the site's paper/retro background.
   - Keep the full item visible in the product card and PDP main frame.

2. Printful views
   - Download every useful available view from Printful for the selected product/variant.
   - Examples: front, back, left, right, side, sleeve, flat, model, detail, inside label, framed view.
   - Use these views before generated lifestyle images in the PDP gallery.

3. Lifestyle images
   - Generate or stage up to 4 lifestyle images per product.
   - Every lifestyle image must use a `1:1` aspect ratio.
   - Generate lifestyle images with the background-removed concept PNG as the primary artwork reference.
   - Use the Printful product mockup as a secondary reference for product shape and print placement.
   - Match the product category and use case.
   - Use believable American 1980s-1990s environments, styling, props, lighting, and daily-life activities.
   - Examples:
     - Mug: diner counter, desk refill, kitchen shelf, gift setup.
     - Apparel: period-appropriate American models in natural daily-life scenes, worn front, worn back, folded detail, or outfit scene. A front-facing pose is not required.
     - Wall art: room wall, desk wall, framed shelf, close-up print detail.
     - Bags: carried view, packed desk, shopping run, detail/strap.
   - Do not generate lifestyle images that alter the real print/artwork.

4. PDP hero lifestyle/story frame
   - Use one product-related lifestyle image that fits the left-side frame tightly.
   - The image must preserve the actual product/artwork and avoid distortion, awkward crop, clipped product parts, or empty dead space.
   - The right-side title, short description, bullet points, production details, best-for text, and artwork/context copy must be rewritten for the exact product.
   - Do not reuse generic copy from another product.

5. Detail images
   - Add close-ups when relevant.
   - Show print quality, material, texture, finish, handle, frame, stitching, or label.

## Background Rules

- Do not use product mockups with a raw white rectangle against the site background unless the frame intentionally calls for it.
- Prefer transparent PNG/WebP for primary isolated product mockups.
- If transparency is impossible, crop and frame the white background intentionally so it reads as a designed card, not an accidental export.
- Shadows are allowed only when they help the product sit naturally in the frame.

## Generated Lifestyle QA

Reject or regenerate lifestyle images if:

- Product artwork/text is changed.
- Green chroma-key remains in the artwork applied to the product.
- Product shape is warped.
- Print placement is wrong.
- The background-removed concept PNG was not used as the primary artwork reference.
- The output is not `1:1`.
- The environment does not credibly reflect everyday American life in the 1980s-1990s.
- Important product parts are clipped.
- The image is off-center for its intended frame.
- The crop breaks the PDP polaroid/lookbook layout.
- The background competes with the product.
- The image looks like a generic stock scene instead of the actual product.

## PDP Gallery Rules

- The gallery should include all useful product views and lifestyle/detail images.
- Label thumbnails in clear order:
  - `01 Front`
  - `02 Back`
  - `03 Left`
  - `04 Right`
  - `05 Lifestyle`
  - `06 Detail`
- The thumbnail rail must scroll when there are more images than fit.
- If thumbnails overflow vertically, show a clear scroll-down button.
- After scrolling down, either show a scroll-up button or keep native scrolling obvious.
- Do not hide extra product images without a cue.

## Layout QA

Before considering a product finished, verify:

- Desktop PDP main image fits the frame.
- Mobile PDP image fits the frame.
- Thumbnail rail can reach every image.
- Lookbook cards do not crop the main subject badly.
- Lifestyle section image and copy align without awkward whitespace.
- PDP hero lifestyle/story image fits its frame and the right-side copy matches the exact product.
- Cart thumbnails still show recognizable products.
- Product grid card image is not distorted.

## Minimum Acceptable Product Image Package

For a new product to go live:

- 1 transparent or intentionally framed primary mockup.
- All available useful Printful views.
- At least 1 lifestyle image generated with the background-removed concept PNG as its primary artwork reference.
- Every lifestyle image is `1:1`.
- Up to 4 lifestyle images when the product is important, expensive, visual, or hero-worthy.
- Thumbnail scroll control if the PDP gallery overflows.

## Complete Standard Product Image Package

Target exactly 10 storefront images:

1. Printful/product front view.
2. Printful/product back or secondary view.
3. Printful/product left or side view.
4. Printful/product right or alternate view.
5. Product detail view.
6. PDP hero lifestyle/story image.
7. Lifestyle/lookbook image 1.
8. Lifestyle/lookbook image 2.
9. Lifestyle/lookbook image 3.
10. Lifestyle/lookbook image 4.

Download additional useful Printful views when the selected product provides them. The background-removed transparent concept PNG is a production asset used for printing and lifestyle generation; it is not counted among the 10 storefront images.

## Preferred Order In PDP

1. Front/product primary.
2. Back or secondary official view.
3. Left/right/side official views.
4. Detail official view.
5. Lifestyle 1.
6. Lifestyle 2.
7. Lifestyle 3.
8. Lifestyle 4.
9. Bundle/context/add-on image if relevant.
